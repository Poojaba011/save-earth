Types of Pollution

🌫️ Air Pollution

Air pollution is the contamination of the indoor or outdoor atmosphere by harmful chemical, physical, or biological agents—such as gases, smoke, and dust—that modify the natural characteristics of the atmosphere.

💧 Water Pollution

Water pollution is the contamination of water bodies (lakes, rivers, oceans, groundwater) by harmful substances like chemicals, waste, and bacteria, rendering water unsafe for use.

🧴 Plastic Pollution

Plastic pollution is a $1.5 trillion global health and environmental crisis, with over 8.3 billion metric tons produced since 1907, of which less than 10% has been recycled. It breaks down into harmful microplastics, contaminating food and water, with annual production on track to triple by 2060.
