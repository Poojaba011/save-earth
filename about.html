About Environment

The environment encompasses all living (biotic) and non-living (abiotic) things—air, water, soil, plants, animals,and built surroundings—that interact to support life on Earth. It is vital for survival, providing resources, but faces critical threats like pollution, deforestation, and climate change. Conservation efforts focus on sustainability, waste reduction, and biodiversity protection.
